Profile

A face built for the front row

Supriya Keshri is a model, actor and content creator who splits her time between Mumbai and Vancouver. Her work moves easily between editorial fashion, commercial campaigns and the runway.

A career highlight came when she landed the cover of Femina, one of India's longest-running fashion and lifestyle titles — a feature that's stayed central to her portfolio ever since. She's also stepped behind the camera as an associate director, and founded her own venture, Supriya Fashion.

On screen, she's currently filming Lohna with RM Films, with Bombay Romance also in production.

"Every shoot is a different character — the job is finding the truth in it."

In The Press

On the cover of Femina

Supriya's cover feature for Femina remains one of the clearest signals of her range in front of the camera — moving from poised editorial stillness to the energy she brings to runway and screen work.

It's the kind of placement that opened doors across fashion and entertainment, and it's where most people are introduced to her work for the first time.
